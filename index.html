FAQ

Common questions

Do you offer free quotes?

Yes. Every estimate is free with no obligation. Call or text (352) 446-2887 or use the form below and we'll get back to you quickly.

Do you handle both painting and pressure washing?

Yes — we're one team for both trades. Many customers bundle them: a thorough wash followed by a fresh coat of paint for a complete exterior refresh.

Can you service residential and commercial properties?

Absolutely. We work on homes, rentals, offices, warehouses, and other commercial spaces — and we're licensed and insured.

How do I request an estimate?

Use the quote form on this page, or call or text us. Tell us a little about your project and we'll schedule a time to take a look.

Do I need to provide the paint or materials?

No — we can supply all equipment and quality materials for the job. If you have a specific paint or color in mind, we're glad to work with that too.

Can pressure washing help prepare surfaces for painting?

Yes. Washing removes dirt, mildew, and loose material so paint adheres properly and lasts longer. It's one of the most important prep steps for exterior painting.
